About HTTPS warnings

If you try to open a secure (https://) site, your browser may show a security warning because this local portal uses a self-signed certificate. To continue to the learning platform:

  1. Tap or click Advanced or Details on the warning screen.
  2. Choose Proceed to 10.42.0.1 (unsafe) or similar to continue to the portal.

This is expected for offline, local networks. Do not proceed on public networks that you don't control.
